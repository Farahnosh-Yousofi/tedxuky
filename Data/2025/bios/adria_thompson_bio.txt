Adria Thompson
Speech-Language Pathologist
Adria Thompson, MA, CCC-SLP, is a speech-language pathologist and lecturer in the Communication Disorders program at Eastern Kentucky University. With 10 years of experience in long-term care communities, she specializes in dementia education and caregiver support. As the founder of Be Light Care Consulting, Adria has created over 500 educational videos that provide practical, evidence-based strategies to improve dementia care. Her social media platforms have grown to over 400,000 followers, offering compassionate and accessible education to help families navigate the challenges of dementia.
