Marc Cormier
Performance Psychologist Specialist
Dr. Marc Cormier is an award-winning performance psychology specialist with nearly 20 years of experience working in elite environments. He is an Associate Professor and Director of the Sport and Exercise Psychology Graduate Program at the University of Kentucky and serves as the Director of Performance Psychology for UK Athletics. Throughout his career, he has worked with numerous NCAA championship teams, as well as All-American and Olympic athletes/champions. More recently, he has been invited to speak to audiences in various non-sport performance contexts such as surgeons, performance artists, emergency responders, and Fortune 500 leaders.
