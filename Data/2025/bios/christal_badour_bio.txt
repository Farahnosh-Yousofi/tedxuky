Christal Badour
Clinical Psychologist
Dr. Christal Badour is a clinical psychologist, researcher, educator, and staunch advocate for survivors of violence and other forms of trauma. She co-founded the University of Kentucky Clinic for Emotional Health, which offers free science-backed therapy in line with the value that all people should have access to the highest quality mental health care, regardless of their identity, culture, geographical location, insurance status, or ability to pay. Dr. Badour uses her platform as an Associate Professor to support the next generation of young clinicians, scholars, and activists to develop the tools necessary to challenge systems that perpetuate violence and create barriers to recovery.
